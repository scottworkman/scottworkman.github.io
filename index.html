Who am I?

I received my Ph.D. from the Computer Science Department at the University of Kentucky in 2018. During this time, I worked as a Research Assistant in the Center for Visualization & Virtual Environments under the supervision of Dr. Nathan Jacobs.

Research Overview

My research interests lie at the boundary of computer vision and machine learning, specifically focused on understanding the connections between images and their geo-temporal context.
